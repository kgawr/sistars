What makes our school special?

Knowledge

We are strong believers in the motto: ''Learning is a life-long journey'', therefore beside the fact that 3 of Us graduated from either English and Polish Universities with an MA in English Philology and PGCE we also hold a BA in Spanish Philology, political studies, music education, acting and we are still broadening our horizons by participating in variuos courses. We learn on an everyday basis to bring the highest possible teaching standards to our students.

Experience

With over a decade of experience in teaching we witnessed the development of our students and we are confident that years spent teaching in public and private schools in Poland and abroad, preparing our students for primary school and high school exams like A-level in English or other international exams like FCE and introducing introducing the most innovative teaching methods and our happy students are a proof that we are a very effective and most sought educational centre in the area with a great potential to expand our services in other European countries.

Atmosphere

Our school is based in our family home. We put all our efforts to make our students feel welcome, comfortable and safe. We are professional pedagogues and we always put out students' well-being first. We care deeply about teacher - student relationships. We hope that our students can really feel our homely atmosphere.

Our projects

Drama

We offer ''daily English camps '' where we teach English through theatre. We use different methods, where kids create their own scripts about an English-speaking country. At the end of a weekly course the kids perform in front of the parents.


The cost of the course : 160 Euros

Euroweek

Euroweek is a School of Leaders, where kids and adults have a chance to meet volunteers from all over the world. They can improve not only their English speaking skills but also public speaking, while learning, for example, traditional dances or other aspects of the culture of the country of the particular volunteer. The camp takes place in a beautiful scenery of the polish mountains. Last year we went to Lądek Zdrój and we learned about the traditional dances of Zimbabwe.

Earth Camp in Salisbury

Every Year in the Summer we enhance our teaching skills by taking part in teacher training in the boarding school in Salilsbury during the Summer camp for kids from all over the world. If you would like to come with Us let Us know. There is an opportunity to take part in the camp as well.
