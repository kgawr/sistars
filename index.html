Welcome to Sistars !

An Educational Centre run with passion

We are a family-run school of languages, we are 3 sisters and we try for our students to feel like they are at home. We collaborate with native speakers from different countries.

Read More

What makes our school special?

Knowledge

We strongly believe in the phrase "life-long learning" therefore beside the fact that we finished Master's degree in English philology with teaching specialisation we also completed Spanish philology, political studies, music education, acting and we are still broadening our horizons by participating in variuos courses. We learn on an everyday basis to bring the highest possible teaching standards to our students.

Experience

With over a decade of experience in teaching we witnessed the development of our students and we are confident that years spent teaching in public and private schools in Poland and abroad, preparing our students for primary school and high school exams like "Matura" or other international exams like FCE and introducing the newest teaching methods work and our satisfied students are the best proof.

Atmosphere

Our school is based in our family home. We put all our efforts to make our students feel welcome, comfortable and safe. We are professional pedagogues and we always put out students' well-being first. We care deeply about people. Our students can literally feel at home.
